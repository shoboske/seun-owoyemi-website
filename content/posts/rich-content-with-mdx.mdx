MDX supports to import React component and embed it directly as markdown content.

## Instagram with react-instagram-embed

<p>
  <InstagramEmbed
    url="https://www.instagram.com/p/Zw9o4/"
    maxWidth={200}
  />
</p>

## YouTube with react-youtube

<p className="youtube-container">
  <YouTubeEmbed url={"https://youtu.be/KP0U3I-f9-Y"} />
</p>

## Twitter with react-twitter-embed

<p>
  <TwitterEmbed url="https://twitter.com/PixelAndBracket/status/1356633038717923333" />
</p>
